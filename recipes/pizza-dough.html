Pizza Dough Recipe

Description

Easy to make and great tasting this recipe is a favorite go-to for homemade pizza!

Ingredients

  • 1 pkg. dry yeast
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 1/2 cups flour

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 425F
  2. In medium bowl, combine yeast and water
  3. Cover 5 minutes
  4. Add remaining ingredients
  5. Mix with large spoon, folding dough together (beat 20 times)
  6. Cover and let rest 5 minutes
  7. With oiled hands spread dough on pizza pan
  8. Top as desired
  9. Bake for 20-25 minutes until cheese melted and edges slightly brown
  10. Let cool 2 minutes, cut, eat, and enjoy!
