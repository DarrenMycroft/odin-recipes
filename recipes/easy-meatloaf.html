Easy Meatloaf

Description

This meatloaf doesn't take long to make at all, and it's very good!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup dried bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp prepared mustard
  1. Preheat the oven to 350F. Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, milk, bread crumbs, and eggs in a large bowl; season with salt and perpper. Transfer into prepared loaf pan.
  3. Mix ketchup, brown sugar, and mustard together in a small bowl unitl well combined; pour over meatloaf and spread it evenly over the top.
  4. Bake in preheated oveer until no longer pink in the center, about 1 hour.
